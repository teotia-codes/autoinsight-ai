This dataset is related to employee attrition and workforce analytics.

Business goals:
- Understand why employees leave
- Identify factors influencing attrition
- Detect class imbalance in attrition
- Evaluate salary, overtime, satisfaction, and job role effects
- Assess whether the dataset is suitable for attrition prediction

Important analytical focus:
- Attrition as target variable
- Employee satisfaction and work-life balance
- Overtime and job involvement
- Salary and years at company
- Department and job role distribution
- Feature importance for attrition prediction
